MakerGirl brings 3D printing, Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) to life for girls to start creating anything they want in their lives. MakerGirl sessions are led by university STEM women for girls ages 7-10 years-old. All of our sessions teach young girls 3D printing, while also teaching them about women leaders in STEM fields, innovative companies, cutting edge technology, and much more. We want your MakerGirl to come to our session and deport wanting to actively participate in any and all parts of STEM!






Mission

Inspire girls to be active in STEM

Vision

Girls live and dream as unstoppable forces that say yes to the challenges of the future which leads to gender equality in all workplaces
